Cloud-based and Offline-enabled Solution


CyberVet is a cloud-based and offline-enabled software. This means your data are stored on our server and accessed using internet connection. This approach saves you the cost of maintaining a server in your veterinary clinic. However, whether there is internet connection or not, CyberVet is designed to let you keep workflow going. It works when there is no internet connective and synchronise when there is, to provide the full digital experience to the least developed market.

Go Paperless, Increase Profitability


Our software helps you digitize administrative task and speed up veterinary diagosis. With limited paper work, improved patient health information availabilty, organised animal health history and reduced medical errors, the veterinarian’s workload is reduced, patient will also be effectively diagnosed and your clinic’s productivity will increased. Ultimately, you reduce cost while providing high quality and better patient care.
